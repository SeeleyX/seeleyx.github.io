03-23 physics simulations

in my third year of studies, i took a module in rigid and deformable bodies. this inspired me to simulate a double pendulum using numerical methods. i first created the pendulum in python using matplotlib but to give me more freedom on the design and customisation of the variables involved, i recreated the simulation on javascript. later on in summer i wanted to investigate 3D simulations with three.js leading me to create a bead falling down a helix with differing radii, at varying gravities. lastly i wanted to try graphing functions without using any libraries, eventually creating a "rollercoaster" that simulated a point riding a polynomial.
